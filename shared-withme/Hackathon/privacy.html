Privacy Policy & Terms and Conditions

Teamify respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, 
and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").
                

Information We Collect:

                

  • Registration Information: When you register for an account, we collect information such as your email 
     address, username, and location (city/state).
    • 
                
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information in your profile, such as your sports 
     interests, skill level, and preferred activities.
    • 
                
  • Location Information: We may collect your location information with your consent to enable features like 
     finding nearby sports enthusiasts or events.
    • 
                
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your use of the App, such as the features you access, the 
     teams you join, and the searches you perform.
    • 
                
    How We Use Your Information:
    
                
  • Provide and Improve the App: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the App, including
     connecting you with other users, facilitating team formation, and finding local sports events.
    • 
     
  • Personalize Your Experience: We may use your information to personalize your experience by 
     recommending content, teams, or athletes that align with your interests.
    •  
     
  • Communication: We may use your information to communicate with you about the App, such as sending
     notifications about upcoming events, team updates, or changes to our policies.
    •  
                
    Information Sharing and Disclosure:
    
We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following 
limited circumstances:
                
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the
     App, such as cloud storage providers or data analytics services. These providers are obligated to maintain 
     the confidentiality of your information.
    • 
                
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court 
     order or subpoena.
                
  • Mergers and Acquisitions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be
     transferred to the successor company.
    • 
                
    Your Choices and Controls:
    
                
  • You can access and update your profile information at any time through the App settings.
    • 
                
  • You can control your location sharing permissions in your device settings.
    • 
                
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in 
     those communications.
    • 
                
    Data Security:
    
We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or 
destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.
                
    Children's Privacy:
    
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information 
from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with 
personal information, please contact us.
                
    Changes to this Privacy Policy:
    
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new 
Privacy Policy on the App.
                
    Contact Us:
    
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Teamify@gmail.com
                
    Code of Conduct for Teamify
    
Teamify is a platform for sports enthusiasts to connect, share their passion, and participate in sports 
activities. To ensure a positive and respectful environment for all users, we have established this Code 
of Conduct.
                
    Respectful Interaction:
    
                
  • Treat all users with respect, regardless of their skill level, sports interests, or background.
    • 
                
  • Avoid offensive language, harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
    • 
                
  • Disagreements are allowed, but maintain a civil and constructive approach.
    • 
                
    Content Guidelines:
    
                
  • Do not post content that is hateful, violent, sexually explicit, or discriminatory.
    • 
                
  • Avoid posting false information, spam, or irrelevant content.
    • 
                
  • Promote sportsmanship and fair play.
    • 
                
    Team & Event Participation:
    
                
  • Be reliable and communicate clearly when joining or managing teams.
    • 
                
  • Respect team decisions and follow through on commitments.
    • 
                
  • If you organize an event, provide accurate information and ensure it adheres to local regulations.
    • 
                
    Enforcement:
    
We reserve the right to take action against users who violate this Code of Conduct, including removing content, 
suspending accounts, or banning users from the App.
                
    Reporting Violations:
    
If you see any content or behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to us through the App's 
reporting feature.
Thank you for helping us create a positive and inclusive community for sports enthusiasts!
